The tavern

Published: Вт 17 Декабрь 2019
By Ankarion

In Tavern.

Hello and welcome to the tavern, the place where most of the best stories begin. The place where people come seeking for wealth, adventures, their destiny or a story to tell. Some of them do find in a tavern a drunk treasure hunter, others meet an old mysterious man...

Khm... As I was saying, I can tell thousands of stories, but the best of them start in a good old tavern. But what in fact DO we know about that taverns? At least we can tell that all that stories are talking about the same one tavern. I know, I know. There are some serious doubts. How is it possible - you might ask. And that would be the right question. You see, this tavern is moving. From time to time occasional story from different, unexpected places mention that tavern. The only fact we know for sure is that it moves.
